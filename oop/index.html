Help Building Our Future

  • GoClimate - Start your climate journey with us
  • Agility Impact - A community for us who believe in using our Agile skillset to build us a better future.
  • Effective Altruism - Everyone wants to do good, but many ways of doing good are ineffective. The EA community is focused on finding ways of doing good that actually work.
  • ClimateAction.tech - A global community for technologists who want to stop climate change.
  • Agile Sustainability Assessment - Asses the sustainability of your team or organization.
  • Cloud Carbon Footprint calculator - Estimate the carbon emissions of your cloud infrastructure.
  • Website Carbon calculator - Get a fast but very rough estimate of your website's carbon emissions.

Further Reading

  • GoClimate's blog - Lot's of good articles on climate change and how you can help.
  • WWF's climate page - A good starting point for climate change information.
  • The UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals - A good overview of what global challenges we are facing.
